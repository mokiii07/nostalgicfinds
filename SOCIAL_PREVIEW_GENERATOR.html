📸 How to Create Your Social Preview Image

Option 1: Using Free Online Tools (EASIEST)

  1. Go to: Canva.com (Free account)
  2. Create new design → Choose "Facebook Cover" or "Social Media Image"
  3. Design your preview:
    • Add your thrift.png logo or hourglass emoji 🎌
    • Title: "NOSTALGIC FIND - Premium Vintage Thrift Store"
    • Subtitle: "Fresh drops weekly • Timeless style"
    • Add a vintage aesthetic (use vintage filters, fonts, colors)
    • Use colors: Dark backgrounds (#1f2933, #2f3a43) with gold accents (#b88a44)
  4. Download as JPG/PNG (recommended: 1200x630px for Facebook)
  5. Upload to your site (name it: social-preview.jpg)

Option 2: Use Your Product Photo

  1. Take a nice photo of your vintage items (flat lay, styled)
  2. Add text overlay: "NOSTALGIC FIND"
  3. Size: 1200x630px minimum
  4. Save as: social-preview.jpg

Option 3: Professional Alternatives

  • Figma: figma.com (Free, powerful)
  • Adobe Express: adobe.com/express (Free, easy)
  • Pixlr: pixlr.com (Free online editor)

📝 After Creating Your Image:

Follow these steps:
  1. Save your image as: social-preview.jpg (1200x630px)
  2. Place it in your project folder (same folder as index.html)
  3. Commit and push to GitHub:
    git add social-preview.jpg git commit -m "Add social media preview image" git push
  4. Done! Your preview is now live 🎉

🧪 Test Your Social Preview

💡 Pro Tips:
  • Use high contrast between text and background
  • Include your logo/brand prominently
  • Use the vintage/thrift aesthetic
  • Keep text readable even when small
  • Use colors: Dark backgrounds + Gold accents (#b88a44)
  • Add a call-to-action: "Shop Now", "View Collection", etc.
