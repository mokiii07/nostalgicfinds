## 🚀 Make It Even Better - Add Custom Preview Image

### **Step 1: Create Your Preview Image**

**Free Tools to Use:**
- 🎨 **Canva** (canva.com) - EASIEST
- 🎨 **Adobe Express** (express.adobe.com)
- 🎨 **Pixlr** (pixlr.com)
- 🎨 **Figma** (figma.com)

**What to Include:**
1. Your thrift.png logo or emoji (🎌)
2. Title: "NOSTALGIC FIND"
3. Tagline: "Premium Vintage Thrift Store"
4. Subtitle: "Fresh drops weekly • Timeless style"
5. Background: Dark color (#1f2933) with vintage aesthetic
6. Button/CTA: "Shop Now"
7. Accent color: Gold (#b88a44)

**Image Size (important!):**
- Ideal: 1200x630px
- Minimum: 1200x630px
- Format: JPG or PNG

### **Step 2: Save Your Image**

1. Download from your design tool
2. **Name it exactly:** `social-preview.jpg`
3. **Size:** 1200x630px
4. **Format:** JPG

### **Step 3: Add to Your Project**

1. Put the image in your project folder:
   ```
   c:\Users\markn\OneDrive\Desktop\New folder (2)\
   ├── index.html
   ├── thrift.png
   └── social-preview.jpg  ← Add here
   ```

2. Commit to GitHub:
   ```powershell
   cd "c:\Users\markn\OneDrive\Desktop\New folder (2)"
   git add social-preview.jpg
   git commit -m "Add professional social media preview image"
   git push
   ```

3. Wait 30 seconds for Netlify to deploy ⚡

### **Step 4: Test It**

1. Go to: **https://www.opengraph.xyz/**
2. Enter your URL: `https://nostalgicfinds.netlify.app`
3. See your beautiful preview! 🎉

## 💡 Pro Tips for Social Sharing

### **When Sharing:**
1. Use a compelling caption
2. Add relevant hashtags: #VintageClothing #ThriftStore #VintageStyle #SustainableFashion
3. Include call-to-action: "Shop now", "Browse collection"
4. Share on multiple platforms for reach

### **Best Hashtags to Use:**
```
#NostalgicFind #VintageClothing #ThriftStore #VintageStyle
#RetroFashion #SecondHandFashion #SustainableFashion #VintageThrift
#ShopVintage #ThriftFashion #VintageFashionBlogger #FashionFlip
```

### **Best Times to Post:**
- **Instagram:** Tuesday-Thursday, 11 AM - 1 PM
- **Facebook:** Tuesday-Thursday, 1 PM - 4 PM
- **Twitter:** Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 10 AM
- **TikTok:** 6 AM - 10 AM, 7 PM - 11 PM

### **Content Ideas to Share:**
1. New item arrivals
2. Behind-the-scenes
3. Styling tips
4. Customer photos
5. Sale announcements
6. Story behind items
